Our Donate Plan

Medium-term needs will include a recovery and reconstruction effort to repair the damaged infrastructure
(river embankments, houses, safe drinking water sources, sanitation facilities, etc.

Food for a family

Raw and ready to eat food that may have come into contact with flood water is risky – this would include raw fruits and vegetables

Shelter for a family

The flood inundation has submerged houses, wells, latrines and agricultural lands disrupting basic services and forcing families to seek shelter.

Education for a child

Your donations also help us support continued education for children during and after floods and cyclones, providing learning support via makeshift schools, and distributing emergency learning kits.

Their lives depend on your donation

Your donations can protect children and families against waterborne diseases through the immediate supply of hygiene kits, (including soaps, toothpaste, toothbrush, comb, towels, sanitary pads, bucket, etc.), jerrycans (water containers) and water purification tablets that provide immediate relief on the ground.
